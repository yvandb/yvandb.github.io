# Cheese Soufflé

[750g](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3LxxKD5QLp0)


g | Ingr
=== | ===
60g | butter
60g | flour
40cl 400g | milk
salt/pepper/nutmeg | enough salt
120g | comté gruyère
6 | egg yolks
6 | egg whites


- roux blanc
- add cold milk, heat and stir until thick
- add spices + egg yolks
- stiffen up egg whites
- 20 mins 180c 360f indiv ramekins
