# Notenbrood

[Stefan Elias](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GXhTVxCNpac)

Ingredient | g
--- | ---
bread flour | 300
whole wheat flour | 200
water | 310
salt | 10
yeast | 7
butter | 20
walnuts | 160
extra water | 30-40

## Steps
- mix for 10 min without nuts+extra water
- mix in nuts+extra water
- rest 60 min
- shape to give power
- divide by 2 x 500g
- roll into balls, flatten
- rise for 60 min
- flour top and score
- 200gC 390gF 25 min
